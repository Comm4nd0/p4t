Day Care & Dog Walking

At Paws 4 Thought we offer group walking on a regular basis.

Dogs need to have fun but we ensure this never becomes out of control. To ensure a well managed walk we will only ever take a maximum of 5 dogs per walk. The dogs are walked in a variety of locations and can be taken swimming if allowed.

Doggy Day Care. We pride ourselves on treating your dog as though they were our own, so you can relax and have full confidence knowing that your dog is receiving the best possible care in our home from home safe and secure environment. Places are limited as we only take 10 day care dogs per day. If you are interested please do get in touch and we'll do our best to accommodate your needs.

All of our full-time Day Care dogs get walked at least once a day and have lots of cuddles. They will also have fun with Claire’s two dogs JD & Leo, who despite their age still both love to play! We try to stick to your dog’s regular routine as much as possible to reduce any stress so you can relax knowing they’re happy and well cared for.

As a dog lover and owner, Claire knows all too well how worrying it can be leaving your dog. Therefore you can always arrange to come for a walk with Claire beforehand, or book a trial day, so you and your dog can get to know her and the other dogs first.
